Bluetooth Beacon

A small BLE beacon tag for attachable and portable assets.

Small Portable Beacon Tag

It is a small BLE beacon for attachable and portable assets such as trailers, drills or chainsaws. When Bluetooth beacon goes close to an M5000, the asset that has the Bluetooth beacon attached will be identified as attached to M5000 on the admin website. You can use M5 Installer to scan for assets with Bluetooth Beacon installed.

Waterproof

Water will never be an issue of losing track of your assets. Ever. Even if your asset is facing heavy rain and flood, your asset can still be picked up by M5000 and the admin website.

Responsive Broadcast

Once a Bluetooth Beacon is activated, it sends broadcast data almost every second.

Long Battery Life

Each M5 Bluetooth Beacon can live for 5 years without replacing battery once it has been activated.

Easy to Configure and Detect

After a Bluetooth Beacon is installed to an asset, all the configurations can be done with just a few clicks on our M5 Installer App. All you need to do is scan for the tracker that is close to you, select an asset, enter the placement of the tracker and tap install. The app will log the installation to the server and the admin website will identify your device.

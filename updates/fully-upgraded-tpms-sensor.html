Fully Upgraded: TPMS Sensor

23/1/2019

Our direct TPMS sensor has been completely upgraded! As you can see in the picture below, the new sensor is of the type that adheres securely to the inside surface of the tyre. This means that the sensor does not interfere with the wheel in any way and has been designed to tolerate all types of additives it may be submerged in. Highly recommended for heavy industry, mining etc. â€‹Symtech also has available a sensor design more specifically for road transport and buses. This unit is held securely in place by a strap around the inner part of the wheel. It can't interfere with the wheel in any way, even when changing the tyre.

Why we made the change:

  • The old design, which involves attaching the sensor to the valve, had several problems which have now been mitigated. Valve types differ from vehicle to vehcile, so the sensor would have to be resized to fit certain wheels. The new designs, fitted with an adhesive patch or metal belt, are universal and can be fitted to any wheel no matter the size.
  • The metal belt and adhesive pad minimize vibrations, which take their toll on the sensor over time. The new designs are more durable and have a longer lifespan.
  • Now located away from the edge of the wheel, the new TPMS doesn't interfere with any work being done to the tyres.

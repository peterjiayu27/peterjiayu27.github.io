M5000

The standard tracking system.

Basic Fleet Tracker

The M5000 is our standard tracking system. It is ideal for companies who want to track the vehicles in their fleet and only require a system with basic features to do so. If you are looking for a complete tracking and management system with a greater range of features, the M5 Fleet Management system.

My Dashboard

My Dashboard is your personalised control panel and go-to place for all summaries of data. It provides you with an overview of the locations and statuses of your vehicles.

Tracking History

The system tracks and stores data on a cloud-based system, allowing you to see where any vehicle has been over a particular period. You can also see details such as vehicle speed and exact location.

Operational Hours

​This feature records the hours spent in operation of all vehicles that have the M5000 system installed. By logging in to the M5 Dashboard, you can see exactly when the vehicle has operated and for how long. You can also set up an alert to remind management or drivers to perform routine maintenance on the vehicle such as changing a filter. You can programme the system to send you a reminder after a specified number of hours in operation.

Distance Travelled

​Our system records the start and stop locations of frequent journeys and uses this information to calculate the distance and to determine the best route. This allows you to compare similar trips and highlight inefficiencies in driving, while being recommended the most ideal route. The stopping time is also recorded, showing where drivers are spending their time. You can also see the exact location and status of any vehicle at any time.​
